Site Name

The name of the company is Razor's Edge Budgeting Education. This name was chosen because many people live life on the razor's edge financially. Our site will educate people about budgeting and provide budgeting tools that will help them take a razor, cut the red out of their budget, and live comfortably.

Site Purpose

The site will provide education about the value and process of budgeting budgeting. It will provide the user an opportunity to take a quiz to test their budgeting knowledge. It will provide a tool to create a budget and keep track of spending.

Color Schema

Primary Color - Emerald

Emerald will be used for headers

Secondary Color - Slate Blue

Slate Blue will be used for the backgrounds of buttons or elements meant to stand out

Accent Color - Soft Gold

Soft Gold will be used for the backgrounds of buttons or elements meant to stand out.

Background Color - Cool Slate Grey

Cool Slate Grey will replace the white background for the entire site.

Primary Text Color - Charcoal Black

Charcoal Black will be used for the primary text in the document. It is good for use on all of the primary, secondary, accent, and background colors. It will primarily be used on the background color.

Secondary Text Color - Graphite Grey

Graphite grey will be used sparingly when the text is not as important. It will be used on the footer. It can be used on the secondary and accent colors.

Inverse Text Color - Ivory White

Ivory White will be used for buttons and cards that are intended to stick out. It will be used on the header. It can be used with the primary, secondary, and accent color.
