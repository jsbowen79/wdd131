About Me

My name is Joseph. I was born in Salt Lake City Utah, but I have spent most of my life in the Mile High City (Denver, Colorado). I spent 16 years serving my community as a law enforcement officer with the Denver Sheriff Department. Due to work related injuries, I am transitioning to a career in computer programming. I enjoy working with computers and I hope to make it a satisfying new career.

Commerce City, Colorado

Commerce City is a suburb just north of Denver. In fact, I live only a few miles from Denver International Airport. Commerce City is the home to the Suncor refinery, the Rocky Mountain Arsenal National Wildlife Refuge, and Dick's Sporting Goods Park which is one of the largest soccer complexes in the United States. It is also the home of the championship golf course Buffalo Run Golf Course. I live only 2 blocks off of the golf course.
